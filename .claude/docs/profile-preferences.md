# Profile Preferences Template

This is a template. The `/setup` skill will help you write your personalised version.

Once completed, paste the output into **Settings > Profile** in the Claude Desktop app.

---

## What goes here

Profile Preferences describe **you** — they apply to every Claude conversation (Chat, Cowork, everything). They are not about your business or your workspace.

## Template

```
My name is [YOUR_NAME]. I am a [YOUR_ROLE] based in [YOUR_LOCATION].

[1-2 sentences about what you do professionally]

Communication preferences:
- [UK English / US English] only
- [Describe your preferred tone — e.g., "Direct and factual", "Friendly but professional", "Formal"]
- [Any specific language to avoid — e.g., "No sycophancy", "No filler phrases", "No emojis unless asked"]
- [How detailed should responses be — e.g., "Concise", "Thorough", "Match the complexity of the question"]
```

## Setup questions the wizard will ask

1. What is your name?
2. What is your role or title?
3. Where are you based?
4. What do you do professionally? (1-2 sentences)
5. Do you use UK English or US English?
6. How do you prefer Claude to communicate with you? (tone, detail level, things to avoid)
