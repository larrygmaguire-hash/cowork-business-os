# Copy Frameworks

Six core copywriting formulas. Select based on content type, audience awareness level, and objective.

## 1. AIDA (Attention, Interest, Desire, Action)

**Best for:** Landing pages, product launches, top-of-funnel ads, email subject lines.

**Structure:**
1. **Attention** — bold claim, surprising stat, or provocative question
2. **Interest** — expand with specific details, paint the picture
3. **Desire** — make the reader want it (benefits, social proof, outcomes)
4. **Action** — clear, specific CTA

**Example:**
> Most teams spend 4 hours a week on reports nobody reads. [Your Product] generates the same reports in 15 minutes, formatted and ready to share. Teams using it report 60% fewer status meetings. Start your free trial today.

---

## 2. PAS (Problem, Agitation, Solution)

**Best for:** Pain-point ads, sales emails, blog intros, retargeting copy.

**Structure:**
1. **Problem** — name the specific pain the audience feels
2. **Agitation** — make it worse (consequences, missed opportunities, compounding cost)
3. **Solution** — present your offer as the fix

**Example:**
> You know the client data is in there somewhere. Three spreadsheets, two CRMs, and an email thread from last quarter. Every time you need a number, it takes 20 minutes to find it. [Your Product] puts every client interaction in one searchable view.

---

## 3. BAB (Before, After, Bridge)

**Best for:** Retargeting ads, case studies, testimonials, email sequences, bottom-of-funnel content.

**Structure:**
1. **Before** — describe the current painful state
2. **After** — describe the desired state (specific, measurable)
3. **Bridge** — show how your product/service gets them there

**Example:**
> Before: your team spends Friday afternoons compiling weekly reports. After: reports generate automatically at 5pm every Friday. The bridge: [Your Product] connects to your existing tools and produces formatted reports without manual input.

---

## 4. ACCA (Awareness, Comprehension, Conviction, Action)

**Best for:** Educational content, LinkedIn ads, thought leadership, webinar invites.

**Structure:**
1. **Awareness** — surface a problem the audience may not know they have
2. **Comprehension** — explain why it matters (data, examples, implications)
3. **Conviction** — build belief that the solution works (proof, credentials, results)
4. **Action** — specific next step

**Example:**
> 73% of businesses cannot answer "which customers are at risk of leaving" without pulling a manual report. That lag costs the average firm 15% of annual revenue in preventable churn. [Your Company] built a predictive model that flags at-risk accounts 30 days before they leave. See how it works — book a 15-minute demo.

---

## 5. PASTOR (Problem, Amplify, Story, Transformation, Offer, Response)

**Best for:** Sales pages, VSL scripts, long-form landing pages, webinar pitches.

**Structure:**
1. **Problem** — name it precisely
2. **Amplify** — make the cost tangible (time, money, opportunity, stress)
3. **Story** — tell a relatable story (yours, a customer's, or a composite)
4. **Transformation** — show the before/after
5. **Offer** — present what you're selling, clearly
6. **Response** — tell them exactly what to do next

---

## 6. Five Questions Framework

**Best for:** Sales pages, product descriptions, "about" sections.

Answer these five questions in order:

1. **What have you got?** — name the product/service clearly
2. **What will it do for me?** — primary benefit in the reader's words
3. **Why should I trust you?** — credentials, results, proof
4. **What makes you different?** — specific differentiator (not "we care more")
5. **What do I do next?** — one clear action

---

## Selecting a Framework

| Awareness Level | Best Framework |
|----------------|---------------|
| Unaware (don't know they have a problem) | ACCA |
| Problem-aware (know the pain, not the solution) | PAS |
| Solution-aware (know solutions exist, evaluating) | BAB, Five Questions |
| Product-aware (know your product, need a push) | AIDA |
| Most aware (ready to buy, need the final nudge) | PASTOR |
