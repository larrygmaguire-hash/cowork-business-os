# Platform Ad Specs

Character limits, format specs, and formula routing for ad copy across platforms.

## LinkedIn Sponsored Content

### Single Image Ads
- **Headline:** max 70 characters (recommended), 200 max
- **Introductory text:** max 150 characters above the fold, 600 max
- **Description:** max 100 characters
- **Image:** 1200x627px (1.91:1 ratio)

### Carousel Ads
- **Card headline:** max 45 characters
- **Introductory text:** max 150 characters above the fold, 255 max
- **Cards:** 2-10 cards, each with image and headline
- **Image per card:** 1080x1080px (1:1 ratio)

### Formula Routing
| Objective | Formula | Why |
|-----------|---------|-----|
| Education / awareness | ACCA | Surfaces a problem the audience may not know they have |
| Pain-point targeting | PAS | Names and agitates a known problem |
| Authority building | Carousel PASP (Problem, Agitate, Solution, Proof per card) | Each card advances the argument |

### LinkedIn Ad Rules
- Professional tone. No clickbait.
- Specific results over vague promises.
- Include social proof where possible (numbers, logos, names).
- CTA: "Learn More", "Download", "Register" — match to offer.

## Meta Ads (Facebook / Instagram)

### Single Image / Video
- **Primary text:** max 125 characters above "See More", 1,000 max
- **Headline:** max 40 characters (recommended), 255 max
- **Description:** max 30 characters (often truncated)
- **Image:** 1080x1080px (1:1) or 1080x1350px (4:5)
- **Video:** 1:1 or 4:5, under 15 seconds for best performance

### Formula Routing
| Funnel Stage | Formula | Why |
|-------------|---------|-----|
| Top of funnel (awareness, launches) | AIDA | Grabs attention and builds desire |
| Bottom of funnel (retargeting) | BAB | Shows transformation for warm audience |

### Meta Ad Rules
- Conversational tone. Write like a person, not a brand.
- First line is everything — it appears in the feed.
- Emojis acceptable but not required.
- Video: hook in first 3 seconds. Text overlay for sound-off viewing.

## Google Ads (Responsive Search Ads)

### Structure
- **Headlines:** up to 15, max 30 characters each
- **Descriptions:** up to 4, max 90 characters each
- Google mixes and matches automatically

### Headline Types (mix all five)
| Type | Purpose | Example |
|------|---------|---------|
| A — Keyword match | Match search query | "[Product Category] for [Audience]" |
| B — Benefit | Primary value proposition | "Cut Reporting Time by 75%" |
| C — Social proof | Numbers and credibility | "Trusted by 500+ Companies" |
| D — CTA | Direct action | "Start Your Free Trial Today" |
| E — Differentiator | Why you vs competitors | "No Setup. No Contracts." |

### Description Formula
Sentence 1: Primary benefit with specificity.
Sentence 2: Secondary benefit or social proof.
Sentence 3: CTA.

### Google Ads Rules
- Every character counts. No filler.
- Front-load keywords in headlines.
- Pin headline A to position 1 for relevance.
- Include at least 8 headlines and 3 descriptions for RSA optimisation.

## TikTok Ads (Spark Ads / In-Feed)

### Specs
- **Video:** 9:16 (vertical), 15-60 seconds, under 30 seconds performs best
- **Text overlay:** max 100 characters (keep key message under 50)
- **CTA button:** auto-generated from platform options

### Script Structure
1. **Hook (0-3 seconds):** Direct statement or question. No brand intro.
2. **Problem (3-10 seconds):** Name the pain in the audience's words.
3. **Solution (10-20 seconds):** Show the product/solution in action.
4. **Proof (20-25 seconds):** Quick result or testimonial.
5. **CTA (last 5 seconds):** Clear next step.

### TikTok Ad Rules
- Must feel native. Polished ads underperform.
- User-generated content style outperforms branded content.
- Hook must land in first 2 seconds or viewers scroll.
- Completion rate matters more than clicks. Write for watch-through.

## Cross-Platform Checklist

Before submitting any ad copy:

- [ ] Within character limits for the platform
- [ ] Formula applied matches the objective and funnel stage
- [ ] CTA is specific and matches the landing page
- [ ] No prohibited marketing language
- [ ] Social proof included where possible
- [ ] 3 variations per platform (for A/B testing)
