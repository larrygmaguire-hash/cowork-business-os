# Social Media

Platform specs, voice adaptation, and workflow for social media content.

## Voice Adaptation

Read `.claude/company/voice.md` for the base voice. Social media adapts it per platform:

| Platform | Register | Length Target |
|----------|----------|-------------|
| LinkedIn | Professional, thought-leadership | 500-1,500 characters |
| X / Twitter | Sharp, concise, opinionated | Max 280 characters |
| Bluesky | Similar to X, slightly more conversational | Max 300 characters |
| TikTok script | Conversational, direct, spoken cadence | 30-60 seconds |
| YouTube Shorts script | Slightly more polished than TikTok | 30-60 seconds |

## LinkedIn (Primary Platform)

### Post Structure

**Pattern P1 (Hook + Body + CTA):**
- Hook line (stops the scroll)
- 2-4 short paragraphs developing one idea
- CTA or question to drive engagement

**Pattern P2 (Story + Lesson):**
- Brief story or observation (3-5 lines)
- The lesson or insight (2-3 lines)
- Application or question (1-2 lines)

**Pattern P3 (List/Framework):**
- Hook stating the framework or list
- Numbered items (each 1-2 lines)
- Closing line with CTA

### Hook Styles

1. **Contrarian:** "Most [common advice] is wrong."
2. **Specific stat:** "[Number]% of [audience] [surprising fact]."
3. **Personal observation:** "I noticed something this week."
4. **Direct challenge:** "[Audience], stop doing [thing]."
5. **Question:** "Why do [group] still [outdated practice]?"

### LinkedIn Rules
- Line breaks between every 1-2 sentences
- Short paragraphs (1-3 lines displayed)
- No hashtags in body (3-5 at the end if used at all)
- No emoji in the first line
- No external links in the post body (kills reach). Put links in comments.

## X / Twitter

- Max 280 characters
- One idea per tweet
- Threads for longer content (each tweet must stand alone)
- No hashtags unless part of a conversation
- Direct, opinionated tone. Take a position.

## Bluesky

- Max 300 characters
- Similar to X in tone and length
- Slightly more conversational
- Community-oriented

## TikTok Script

**Structure:**
1. **Hook (0-3 seconds):** Direct statement or question. No preamble.
2. **Body (3-45 seconds):** One point, explained clearly. Visual-first thinking.
3. **CTA (last 5-10 seconds):** Follow, comment, or share prompt.

**Script rules:**
- Write for spoken delivery, not reading
- Short sentences. Conversational rhythm.
- Include visual/action cues in brackets: [show screen], [cut to], [text overlay]
- Target 30-60 seconds total

## YouTube Shorts Script

**Structure:**
- Same as TikTok but slightly more polished
- Can reference specific tools, frameworks, or examples
- Include text overlay cues for key points

## Emoji Usage

- Sparingly. One emoji per post maximum.
- Never in the first line of LinkedIn posts.
- Use for visual breaks, not decoration.
- Match the platform culture (more on TikTok, fewer on LinkedIn).

## Hashtag Strategy

- LinkedIn: 3-5 hashtags at the end, or none
- X: only if joining a specific conversation
- TikTok: 3-5 relevant hashtags
- Bluesky: rarely used

## CTA Patterns

| Type | Example |
|------|---------|
| Engagement | "What has your experience been?" |
| DM | "DM me [keyword] and I will send you [thing]" |
| Link (in comments) | "Link in the comments" |
| Follow | "Follow for more [topic]" |
| Save | "Save this for later" |

## Content Pillars

Before creating social content, identify 3-5 content pillars from `.claude/company/overview.md` and `.claude/company/audiences.md`. All social content should map to a pillar. This prevents random posting and builds thematic authority.
