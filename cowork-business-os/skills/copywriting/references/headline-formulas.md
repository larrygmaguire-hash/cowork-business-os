# Headline Formulas

20+ formulas organised by type. Use for headlines, subject lines, ad headlines, and sub-headlines.

## Outcome-Focused

1. **How to [achieve outcome] without [common obstacle]**
   — "How to Cut Reporting Time Without Hiring an Analyst"

2. **[Number] ways to [achieve outcome]**
   — "7 Ways to Reduce Client Churn This Quarter"

3. **The [adjective] way to [achieve outcome]**
   — "The Fastest Way to Onboard New Clients"

4. **Get [desired result] in [timeframe]**
   — "Get Your First Dashboard in 10 Minutes"

## Problem-Focused

5. **Why [common approach] is [negative consequence]**
   — "Why Manual Reporting Is Costing You 8 Hours a Week"

6. **Stop [painful activity]. Start [better alternative].**
   — "Stop Chasing Invoices. Start Getting Paid on Time."

7. **[Number] [audience] mistakes that [negative outcome]**
   — "5 Pricing Mistakes That Kill Your Margins"

8. **Are you still [outdated practice]?**
   — "Are You Still Tracking Projects in Spreadsheets?"

## Audience-Focused

9. **For [specific audience] who [specific situation]**
   — "For Consultants Who Are Tired of Scope Creep"

10. **What [respected group] knows about [topic] that you don't**
    — "What Top-Performing Sales Teams Know About Follow-Up"

11. **[Audience], here is why [surprising claim]**
    — "Founders, Here Is Why Your Best Clients Never Refer You"

## Differentiation

12. **[Common belief] is wrong. Here is what works instead.**
    — "More Features Won't Fix Your Retention. Here Is What Works Instead."

13. **We [did unexpected thing]. Here is what happened.**
    — "We Stopped Cold Emailing for 90 Days. Here Is What Happened."

14. **The [topic] guide that [differentiator]**
    — "The Pricing Guide That Actually Shows You the Numbers"

## Proof-Focused

15. **How [company/team] [achieved result] in [timeframe]**
    — "How a 3-Person Team Doubled Revenue in 6 Months"

16. **[Number]% of [audience] [do/don't do thing]. Here is why it matters.**
    — "73% of Teams Don't Track Their Meeting Costs. Here Is Why It Matters."

17. **[Specific result] — here is how**
    — "40% Fewer Support Tickets — Here Is How"

## Additional

18. **Everything you need to know about [topic]**
    — "Everything You Need to Know About GDPR for Small Businesses"

19. **[Number] lessons from [experience/period]**
    — "12 Lessons From Running a Remote Team for 3 Years"

20. **The complete guide to [topic]**
    — "The Complete Guide to Client Onboarding"

21. **[Topic]: what nobody tells you**
    — "Hiring Your First Employee: What Nobody Tells You"

## Sub-Headline Formulas

Sub-headlines expand or qualify the main headline:

- **Specificity:** "[Main headline] — for [audience] who [situation]"
- **Proof:** "[Main headline]. [Specific result] in [timeframe]."
- **Mechanism:** "[Main headline]. Using [method/approach]."
- **Objection:** "[Main headline] — even if [common objection]."
