# On-Page SEO Checklist

Apply to all web-published content. Not applicable to ads, emails, or offline materials.

## Focus Keyword Placement

| Location | Requirement |
|----------|------------|
| Page title / H1 | Must contain focus keyword |
| First paragraph | Within the first 100 words |
| At least one subheading | In an H2 or H3 |
| Meta description | Naturally included |
| URL slug | Hyphenated, lowercase, no stop words |
| Image alt text | Keyword in at least one image alt |

## Keyword Density

- Target: 0.5-2.5%
- Natural placement. If it reads as forced, reduce density.
- Do not stuff keywords into headings, alt text, or meta fields unnaturally.

## Meta Description

- Length: 120-155 characters
- Must include focus keyword
- Benefit-focused (what the reader gets, not what the page is about)
- Active voice
- Include a CTA element where appropriate ("Learn how...", "See why...")

## SEO Title

- Length: 50-60 characters
- Front-load the primary keyword where natural
- Include brand name at end if space permits ("[Title] | [Brand]")
- Must be different from the H1 (complementary, not identical)

## Internal Linking

- Minimum 2 contextual internal links per page
- Link to related content (not random pages)
- Use descriptive anchor text (not "click here" or "read more")
- Check that linked pages are live and relevant

## External Links

- At least 1 link to an authoritative external source where appropriate
- Opens in new tab
- Only link to reputable, relevant sources
- No broken external links

## Semantic Keywords

- Include 3-5 related terms naturally in the body
- These are LSI (Latent Semantic Indexing) keywords — terms Google expects to see alongside the focus keyword
- Do not force them. They should appear naturally in well-written content on the topic.

## Heading Hierarchy

- One H1 per page (the page title)
- H2 for main sections
- H3 for subsections within H2s
- No skipped levels (no H1 > H3 without H2)
- Headings should be descriptive and scannable — the reader should understand the page structure from headings alone

## Image Optimisation

- All images have descriptive alt text
- Focus keyword in at least one alt attribute
- Images compressed for web (WebP or optimised JPEG/PNG)
- Width and height attributes set to prevent layout shift
- Lazy loading on below-the-fold images

## URL Slug

- Lowercase, hyphenated
- No special characters, underscores, or spaces
- No stop words (the, and, of, in) unless needed for readability
- Short and descriptive (under 75 characters)
- Consistent format across the site

## E-E-A-T Signals

- **Experience:** First-person perspective where appropriate ("In our experience...")
- **Expertise:** Author credentials or company expertise mentioned
- **Authoritativeness:** Citations, data sources, named experts
- **Trustworthiness:** Accurate claims, no fabricated statistics, clear attribution
