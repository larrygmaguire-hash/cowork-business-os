# Human Resources

> **SETUP REQUIRED:** This file contains default settings. When you first use this folder, Claude will ask you to configure it for your organisation — what you store here, which subfolders you need, and any specific rules or conventions. Update this file with your answers so Claude follows your preferences in future sessions.

## Purpose

People management — recruitment, onboarding, policies, training, and employee records.

## Subfolders

| Folder | Use For |
|--------|---------|
| `02.1 Recruitment/` | Job descriptions, candidate tracking, interview notes |
| `02.2 Onboarding/` | New starter checklists, induction materials, welcome packs |
| `02.3 Policies/` | HR policies, handbooks, codes of conduct |
| `02.4 Training/` | Training plans, course materials, development records |
| `02.5 Employee Records/` | Personnel files, contracts, performance reviews |

## Configuration

**Status:** Unconfigured — using defaults.

Customise the following for your organisation. Delete or update each item once decided:

- **What goes here vs Projects:** Standing HR processes and reference material belong here. Time-bound HR initiatives (e.g., a recruitment drive, a policy overhaul) may be better suited to `Projects/` with a project ID.
- **What goes here vs Clients:** If your business provides HR services to clients, client-specific HR work belongs in `Clients/[client-name]/`. This folder is for your own internal HR.
- **Subfolders:** Add, rename, or remove subfolders to match your needs. The defaults above are suggestions. Numbering and table updates are handled automatically — see `.claude/rules/file-conventions.md`.
