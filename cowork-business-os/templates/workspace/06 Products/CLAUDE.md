# Products

> **SETUP REQUIRED:** This file contains default settings. When you first use this folder, Claude will ask you to configure it for your organisation — what you store here, which subfolders you need, and any specific rules or conventions. Update this file with your answers so Claude follows your preferences in future sessions.

## Purpose

Product management — catalogue, development, and pricing for what the business sells.

## Subfolders

| Folder | Use For |
|--------|---------|
| `06.1 Catalogue/` | Product listings, descriptions, specifications, feature matrices |
| `06.2 Development/` | Product roadmaps, feature requests, development notes |
| `06.3 Pricing/` | Pricing models, rate cards, discount structures |

## Configuration

**Status:** Unconfigured — using defaults.

Customise the following for your organisation. Delete or update each item once decided:

- **What goes here vs Projects:** Standing product information (catalogue, pricing) belongs here. Product development initiatives (e.g., launching a new product line) may be better suited to `Projects/` with a project ID.
- **Services businesses:** If your business sells services rather than physical products, consider renaming this folder to `Services/` and adjusting subfolders accordingly.
- **Subfolders:** Add, rename, or remove subfolders to match your needs. The defaults above are suggestions. Numbering and table updates are handled automatically — see `.claude/rules/file-conventions.md`.
- **Naming conventions:** See `.claude/rules/file-conventions.md` for file and folder naming rules.
- **Heavy files:** Product images, technical drawings, and video demos should remain on cloud storage. Reference them by path.
