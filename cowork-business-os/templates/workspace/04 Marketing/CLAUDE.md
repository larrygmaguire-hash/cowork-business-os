## Purpose

Brand, campaigns, content creation, social media, and marketing analytics.

## Subfolders

| Folder | Use For |
|--------|---------|
| `04.1 Campaigns/` | Campaign briefs, plans, performance tracking |
| `04.2 Content/` | Blog drafts, newsletters, copywriting, editorial calendars |
| `04.3 Brand Assets/` | Brand guidelines, logos, colour palettes, tone of voice docs |
| `04.4 Analytics/` | Marketing reports, conversion data, channel performance |
| `04.5 Social Media/` | Social media calendars, post drafts, platform strategies |

## Configuration

**Status:** Unconfigured — using defaults.

Customise the following for your organisation. Delete or update each item once decided:

- **What goes here vs Projects:** Ongoing marketing functions (brand guidelines, content calendars) belong here. Specific campaigns with deadlines may be better suited to `Projects/` with a project ID.
- **What goes here vs Clients:** If your business is a marketing agency, client campaign work belongs in `Clients/[client-name]/`. This folder is for your own marketing.
- **Subfolders:** Add, rename, or remove subfolders to match your needs. The defaults above are suggestions. Numbering and table updates are handled automatically — see `.claude/rules/file-conventions.md`.
