# Documentation

Shared reference materials and reports that span multiple projects or apply across the workspace.

## Subfolder

- **Reports/** -- generated reports and analyses (status reports, monthly summaries, ad hoc analyses)

## What goes here

- Cross-project reports and summaries
- Reference documents that apply workspace-wide
- Generated outputs that do not belong to a single project

## What does NOT go here

- Project-specific work (use `Projects/P###/` instead)
- Client-specific documents (use `Clients/C###/` instead)
- Department-specific procedures (use the relevant `## Department/` folder)
- Archived completed projects (use `Archive/` instead)
- Reusable templates -- there is no central templates folder. Each project has its own PRD.md generated from a structured pattern by the `creating-projects` skill. Each client has its own PRD.md generated by `creating-clients`. Skills hold their own template patterns.
