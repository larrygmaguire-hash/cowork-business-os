# Document Templates

Reusable document templates that any project or skill can pull from.

## Examples of what goes here

- Proposal templates (structured outline for client proposals)
- Report templates (monthly report format, status report format)
- Brief templates (creative brief, project brief, content brief)
- Meeting templates (agenda templates, minutes templates)
- Engagement letter templates
- Onboarding document templates

## How templates are used

When a skill produces a document (e.g., `drafting-documents`), it can reference a template from this folder and fill in the project-specific content. Templates ensure consistency across deliverables.

## Naming

Use descriptive names: `Proposal-Template.md`, `Monthly-Report-Template.docx`, `Creative-Brief-Template.md`.

Keep templates generic. Place project-specific content in the project folder, not here.
