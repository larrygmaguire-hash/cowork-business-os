# Reports

Generated reports and analyses that span the workspace or apply to multiple projects.

## Examples of what goes here

- Workspace status reports (`/workspace-status` output saved to disk)
- Weekly or monthly summaries
- Cross-project analyses
- Performance reports
- Ad hoc reports requested by stakeholders

## Naming

Use date-prefixed filenames: `2026-04-14-Workspace-Status.md`, `2026-04-Q2-Performance-Summary.md`.

## What does NOT go here

- Project-specific reports (put in the project folder)
- Client-specific reports (put in the client folder)
- Drafts and works in progress (put in the relevant project's `Drafts/`)
