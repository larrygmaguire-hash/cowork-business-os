## What to put in this CLAUDE.md when you create a real project

The `creating-projects` skill generates a CLAUDE.md with:

- **Scope:** what the project covers and what it does not
- **Deliverables:** the specific outputs to produce
- **Stakeholders:** who is involved, who approves, who consumes the output
- **Constraints:** deadlines, budgets, approval requirements
- **Current state:** where the work stands right now
- **Conventions:** any project-specific rules that override the workspace defaults

The full operational context (autonomy levels, file conventions, state management) is inherited from `.claude/CLAUDE.md`.
