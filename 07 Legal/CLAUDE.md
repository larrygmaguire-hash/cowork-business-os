Legal affairs — contracts, regulatory compliance, and intellectual property.

| Folder | Use For |

## Configuration

**Status:** Unconfigured — using defaults.

Customise the following for your organisation. Delete or update each item once decided:

- **What goes here vs Finance:** Tax and financial compliance sits in `01 Finance/01.4 Tax & Compliance/`. Regulatory compliance that is not financial (data protection, industry licensing, health and safety) belongs here.
- **What goes here vs Sales:** Sales contracts may live in `03 Sales/03.3 Contracts/` or here — choose one location and be consistent. The principle is: if Legal owns the document, it goes here; if Sales owns the relationship, it goes there.
- **What goes here vs HR:** Employment contracts and HR-specific policies belong in `02 Human Resources/`. General company policies with legal standing (e.g., data protection policy) may belong here.
