## Purpose

Financial management — accounts payable/receivable, budgets, tax compliance, and financial reporting.

## Subfolders

| Folder | Use For |
|--------|---------|
| `01.1 Accounts Payable/` | Supplier invoices, payment tracking, purchase orders |
| `01.2 Accounts Receivable/` | Client invoices, payment follow-ups, revenue tracking |
| `01.3 Budgets/` | Annual budgets, departmental budgets, forecasts |
| `01.4 Tax & Compliance/` | Tax returns, VAT records, regulatory filings |
| `01.5 Reports/` | Financial statements, P&L summaries, cash flow reports |
